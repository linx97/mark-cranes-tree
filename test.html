About Mark Crane's Tree and Arborist Services in Santa Barbara, CA

Locally owned and operated since 1995. Our company features a highly motivated and well trained staff of certified arborists that are not only eager to assist you with all your tree concerns, they are also local, experienced, and English-speaking individuals.

Our team is equipped to handle everything from clearing large areas of land, removing dying trees and old stumps, and precision pruning of trees on your personal property.

Our Team


Mark Crane

Certified Arborist, Owner

Mark Crane began working on trees in Lake Tahoe in 1987. He is an ISA-Certified Arborist who remains amazed at the trees of Santa Barbara. Each species is so different from the other, and Mark knew that the best thing he could do to was really understand the urban forest. When he started his company, the most important quality he looked for in his team members is a true passion for trees. It is also important that the team learn from each other and participate in healthy competitions that enhance the skills they need on a daily basis. Mark is proud of the team at Mark Crane's Tree and Arborist Services and knows that each person is dedicated to the health and well-being of our client's trees.
